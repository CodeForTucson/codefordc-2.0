Women* Techmakers Tucson Hackathon

November 14 - 15, 2015

Google Development Group, Code for Tucson, and UA Libraries are proud to present the Women Techmakers Tucson Hackathon on November 14-15 at the University of Arizona's Science and Engineering Library.

Join us for a weekend of food, fun, learning, and hacking! This free event provides an opportunity for women of all levels of technical expertise to collaboratively develop tech-driven projects over the course of two days.

All women are encouraged to attend — no computer programming experience required! And projects are never built by ones and zeros alone — designers, product managers, community organizers, writers, and YOU are very much welcome!

Event highlights:

  • Workshops on coding, rapid prototyping (e.g. electronic circuit building and 3D printing), and professional development
  • Mentoring and on-site tech support during project development
  • Professional development opportunities and community partnership occasions
  • Amazing swag, prizes, and food
  • Check out the full agenda at the bottom of this page!
